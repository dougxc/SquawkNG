Provides classes for building and optimizing the control flow graph of a method. For this purpose the front end iterates over the bytecodes twice. First the {@link javac1.frontend.BlockListBuilder} only determines the starts of all basic blocks by examining the destinations of jumps and the successors of conditional jump instructions. Afterwards the {@link javac1.frontend.GraphBuilder} fills the gaps between the basic blocks with instructions.

Overlapping with the construction of the intermediate representation, some inexpensive high-impact optimizations are applied. Each generated instruction is transformed into its canonical form by the {@link javac1.frontend.Canonicalizer}. In addition a simple form of value numbering is used, short static or final methods are inlined, and innermost loops are determined for better register allocation.
